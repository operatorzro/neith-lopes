Most of my edge, such as it is, comes from process rather than prediction. Here's the loop I run every week.

## Capture before you interpret

I separate data capture from interpretation. The first pass is mechanical: economic releases, central-bank commentary, positioning data, and the levels that mattered. No opinions yet — just the tape.

## Compare signals, don't average them

Averaging signals washes out information. I'd rather hold two contradictory reads side by side and ask which one the next data point would invalidate.

## The post-mortem is the product

The trade is almost incidental. What compounds is the review trail: what I expected, what happened, and where the gap was. That's the part Neural Edge was built to make easy.
